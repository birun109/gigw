About the Ministry

Learn about our mandate, mission, organisational structure and responsibilities.

Our Mandate

The Ministry of Digital Governance is responsible for implementing national e-Governance initiatives, ensuring transparency, digital inclusion and secure public service delivery.

Vision & Mission

Vision: Empower citizens through secure and accessible digital services.

Mission: Develop robust digital infrastructure and improve public service efficiency.

Organisational Structure

The Ministry is headed by the Hon’ble Minister and supported by Secretary, Joint Secretaries and technical divisions.
