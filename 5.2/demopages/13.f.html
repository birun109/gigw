Accessibility Violations

  • ❌ Audio autoplay without pause control
  • ❌ No stop mechanism available
  • ❌ No independent volume control
  • ❌ Screen reader users cannot hear speech clearly
  • ❌ Violates WCAG 2.1 – 1.4.2 Audio Control

Evaluator Checklist – Expected Failures

  • ❌ Audio begins automatically on page load
  • ❌ No visible audio control interface
  • ❌ Audio continues indefinitely
  • ❌ Accessibility testing flags violation
