Digital Citizen Services

Access government information and online services easily.

✗ NON-COMPLIANT – Text contrast ratio below WCAG 2.1 minimum (4.5:1)

Online Public Services

Citizens can access digital services including certificates, public grievance systems, and welfare scheme registration through this national portal.

However, the text contrast is insufficient, making the content difficult to read for users with low vision or colour deficiencies.

Failure Summary

  • ✗ Text contrast ratio below 4.5:1
  • ✗ Large text contrast below 3:1
  • ✗ No high contrast mode available
  • ✗ Buttons lack sufficient foreground/background contrast
  • ✗ Links not clearly distinguishable

Evaluator Checklist (Expected Failure)

  • ✗ Verify contrast ratio using WCAG contrast checker tool
  • ✗ Confirm body text fails 4.5:1 requirement
  • ✗ Confirm button text fails 3:1 requirement
  • ✗ Validate non-compliance with WCAG 1.4.3
