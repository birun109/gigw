Government Services Information

Citizens can access multiple government services including certificate applications, welfare schemes, and grievance registration through the national portal. The website layout adapts dynamically when text spacing is increased to support users with visual or cognitive disabilities.

Compliance Summary

  • Flexible containers allow spacing adjustments
  • No fixed heights restricting text expansion
  • Line spacing supports ≥1.5 line height
  • Letter spacing and word spacing supported

Evaluator Verification Checklist
