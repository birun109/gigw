Failure Examples – Structure & Relationships ❌

1. Data Table Without Proper Structure

Scenario: Presenting annual budget allocation but without semantic relationships.

Department 2024 Allocation
Education 500
Health & Family Welfare 850

Why This Fails:
Header cells are created using <td> instead of <th>. No scope attributes are provided. Screen readers cannot programmatically determine relationships between headers and data cells.

2. Radio Buttons Without Grouping

Scenario: Related radio buttons visually grouped but not programmatically associated.

Select Preferred Contact Method
Email
Phone
SMS

Why This Fails:
The heading is only visually styled using a <div>. No <fieldset> or <legend> is used. Each radio button has a different name, so they are not grouped. Screen readers cannot identify that these options belong to a single question.
