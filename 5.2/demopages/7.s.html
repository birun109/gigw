Success Examples – Structure & Relationships

1. Data Tables (Structure and Relationships)

Scenario: Presenting hierarchical data showing annual budget allocation by department.

Annual Budget Allocation (in Crores)
Department 2024 Allocation
Education 500
Health & Family Welfare 850

Compliance Justification:
The use of <th> with scope attributes programmatically establishes relationships between headers and data cells. Screen readers correctly announce row and column context for each data value.

2. Form Field Groups (Relationships)

Scenario: Grouping related radio buttons with a common title.

Select Preferred Contact Method

Compliance Justification:
The <fieldset> element groups related controls programmatically, and the <legend> provides accessible context. Screen readers announce the legend before each radio option, ensuring meaningful relationships.
